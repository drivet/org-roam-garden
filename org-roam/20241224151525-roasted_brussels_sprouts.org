#+title: Roasted Brussels Sprouts

[[id:3a1caf2c-7854-4cf0-bb11-bb7806618c36][Recipes]]

Easy.  Delicious.  Best way to eat Brussels sprouts.

Source: https://cookieandkate.com/perfect-roasted-brussels-sprouts-recipe/

Yield: 4 servings

** Ingredients

 * 1 1/2 pounds Brussels sprouts, trimmed, halved
 * 2 tablespoons extra-virgin olive oil
 * 1/2 teaspoon fine sea salt
 * 1/2 teaspoon black pepper
   
** Instructions

 * Preheat oven to 425 F
 * Put Brussels sprouts in plastic zip lock bag with salt, pepper, and oil.
   Shake well to coat.
 * Line baking sheet with parchment paper.  Spread sprouts on sheet.
 * Bake sprouts for 20-25 minutes, until they are charred and caramelized.
