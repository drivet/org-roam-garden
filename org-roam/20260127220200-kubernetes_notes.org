#+title: Kubernetes Notes

Kubernetes is a distributed computing model used to create microservice
based applications deployed onto a cluster.

** Pods

The pod concept is the atom of kubernetes, the smallest unit that can be
manipulated.  A pod wraps one or more tightly coupled dockers images, but
often only one.

A pod is a single unit of execution.

** Deployment

A Deployment manages the life cycle of your pods.  Pods are created and
destroyed by Deployment object.  When a pod dies, the Deployment is
responsible for creating a new instance.

** Service

A Service fronts a bunch of pods and provides a single point of contact for
a series of ephemeral pods.
