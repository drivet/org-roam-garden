#+title: Rating system

I like the 4 star system.  It's easy to remember.

 * **1 star**: Terrible, would actively avoid
 * **2 stars**: Would not actively avoid, but not great
 * **3 stars**: Good, would drink again
 * **4 stars**: Excellent, among my favourites, would actively seek out.
