Easy and comforting, especially on a cold night.

Yield: 4 servings

** Ingredients
 * 6 cups low-sodium vegetable broth
 * 1 medium yellow onion, diced
 * 1-2 carrots, diced
 * 1-2 celery ribs, diced
 * 2 tablespoons olive oil
 * salt and pepper
 * 1 cup pastina or any tiny pasta variety, like alphabet, orzo, or acini di pepe
 * Grated Parmesan cheese, for serving

** Instructions

 1. Fry the vegetables in the olive oil on medium heat with some and pepper
    to taste, about 7 minutes
 2. Add the broth, simmer for 15 minutes on medium heat.
 3. Turn off the heat, and puree the soup with a hand blender until mostly
    uniform with only a small amount chunkiness remaining.  Add some more
    salt to taste.
 4. Add the pasta, raise temperature to medium high, cook for about 10-15
    minutes.
 5. Ladle into bowls, sprinkle with cheese.
