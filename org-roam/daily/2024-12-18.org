#+title: Daily note (2024-12-18)

The hard part of using emacs and open source tools in general is mapping
what you want to do onto the tools available.

Knowing what you want to do is harder then you might think.  We often don't
have a clear idea of what we want our workflow to be.

The next hardest part is knowing what tools are actually available, and what
they can do.
