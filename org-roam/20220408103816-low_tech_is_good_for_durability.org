#+title: Low tech is good for durability

Low tech solutions last longer, because they are less complicated.

Books have been known to last decades, even centuries, with people still
being able to read them.  Are we confident that Microsoft Word docs or CDs
will last as long?

[[id:a2e09732-e1f9-4e01-a5fa-74092ae75cd5][Plain Text]] in digital form is about as low tech as you can get and still
touch a computer.  No special software is required to make sense of the
content, though special software might be required to process it
efficiently.
