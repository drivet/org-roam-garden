#+title: Gnus Topics

[[id:436197ee-3978-474a-aa6d-373d0cfd2d67][Gnus]]

Topics let you group your groups into a hierarchy.  See Group buffer

T n for a new topic

T m to move a group to a topic

T r to rename a topic

T DEL to delete an empty topic

ESC TAB will "unindent" a top, i.e. move it one level up in the
hierarchy. TAB will move it one level down.
