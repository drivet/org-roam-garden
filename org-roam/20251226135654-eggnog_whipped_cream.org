#+title: Eggnog Whipped Cream
#+filetags:

[[id:3a1caf2c-7854-4cf0-bb11-bb7806618c36][Recipes]]

Good over hot chocolate or as a swiss roll filling

Yield: 12 servings

** Ingredients
 * 1 cups chilled whipping cream
 * 1/4 cups eggnog
 * 1-2 tablespoon powdered sugar
 * 1/2 teaspoon nutmeg

** Instructions

Mix the powdered sugar, nutmeg and cream.  Whip the cream until you get soft
peaks.  Beat in the eggnog at low speed until just mixed.
