#+title: Emacs Faces

[[id:e8f63911-0c0b-4f37-9aed-b2e415144f9d][Emacs]]

You can use ~describe-char~ over a character to see information about how
the character is rendered, including its face.

Once you have the face, you can use ~describe-face~ to see information about
the face.
